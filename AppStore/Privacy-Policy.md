# Modaics Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** February 18, 2026

**Effective Date:** February 18, 2026

---

## Introduction

Modaics ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application Modaics (the "App") and related services.

By using Modaics, you consent to the data practices described in this policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our App.

---

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information You Provide

**Account Information**
- Name and username
- Email address
- Phone number
- Profile photo
- Biographical information
- Location (city/general area)

**Transaction Information**
- Payment method details (processed securely via third-party providers)
- Purchase and sales history
- Shipping addresses
- Transaction receipts

**Listing Information**
- Photos of items you sell
- Item descriptions and details
- Pricing information
- Category and tag selections

**Communications**
- Messages between buyers and sellers
- Customer support inquiries
- Feedback and reviews

### Information Automatically Collected

**Device Information**
- Device type and model
- Operating system version
- Unique device identifiers
- Mobile network information
- IP address

**Usage Information**
- App features you use
- Time spent in the App
- Search queries and filters
- Items viewed and saved
- Interaction patterns

**Camera and Photos**
- With your permission, we access your camera and photo library
- Photos are used for item listings and visual search
- Images may be processed for quality and content moderation

**Location Information**
- General location (city/region) for local marketplace features
- Precise location only with explicit permission
- Used for shipping estimates and nearby item discovery

### Information from Third Parties

- Social media profiles (if you choose to connect)
- Payment processors (transaction confirmations)
- Analytics providers (aggregated usage data)
- Identity verification services (if required)

## How We Use Your Information

### Primary Purposes

1. **Provide and Maintain Services**
   - Process transactions and payments
   - Facilitate communication between buyers and sellers
   - Display personalized content and recommendations
   - Enable visual search and AI features

2. **Improve User Experience**
   - Analyze usage patterns to enhance features
   - Develop new products and services
   - Conduct research and analytics
   - Personalize your experience

3. **Safety and Security**
   - Verify user identities
   - Detect and prevent fraud
   - Monitor for prohibited items
   - Enforce our Terms of Service
   - Protect against harmful or illegal activity

4. **Communication**
   - Send transaction notifications
   - Provide customer support
   - Send marketing communications (with consent)
   - Deliver app updates and security alerts

### AI and Machine Learning

Your photos and usage data help us train and improve our AI features:
- Visual search accuracy
- Auto-listing generation
- Content recommendations
- Fraud detection

**Important**: We do not use your photos to identify individuals or train facial recognition systems.

## How We Share Your Information

### With Other Users

When you engage in transactions:
- Buyers see seller usernames, ratings, and general location
- Sellers see buyer shipping addresses (for fulfillment)
- Profile information visible to other users

### With Service Providers

We share information with trusted third parties who assist us:
- **Payment Processors**: Stripe, PayPal for secure transactions
- **Cloud Services**: AWS, Google Cloud for data storage
- **Analytics**: Firebase, Mixpanel for app improvement
- **Shipping**: Integrated carriers for label generation
- **Customer Support**: Zendesk, Intercom for assistance

### For Legal Reasons

We may disclose information:
- To comply with legal obligations
- To respond to lawful requests from authorities
- To protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property
- To prevent or investigate fraud or illegal activity

### Business Transfers

If Modaics is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale:
- User information may be transferred
- You will be notified of any ownership change
- New privacy practices will be disclosed

## Your Rights and Choices

### Account Management

**Access and Update**
- Edit profile information in app settings
- Update preferences and notification settings
- View transaction history

**Data Portability**
- Request a copy of your data
- Download your listings and purchase history

**Deletion**
- Delete your account at any time
- Remove individual listings
- Request data deletion (subject to legal requirements)

### Privacy Controls

**Location**
- Enable/disable precise location
- Set location visibility preferences

**Notifications**
- Customize push notification settings
- Manage email preferences
- Control marketing communications

**Camera and Photos**
- Grant or revoke camera access
- Manage photo library permissions
- Delete uploaded photos

### California Residents (CCPA)

California residents have additional rights:
- Know what personal information is collected
- Know if personal information is sold or shared
- Opt-out of sale of personal information
- Non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights

To exercise CCPA rights, email: privacy@modaics.com

### European Users (GDPR)

EU residents have the right to:
- Access their personal data
- Correct inaccurate data
- Erase personal data ("right to be forgotten")
- Restrict or object to processing
- Data portability
- Withdraw consent

To exercise GDPR rights, email: privacy@modaics.com

## Data Security

We implement comprehensive security measures:

- **Encryption**: AES-256 for data at rest, TLS 1.3 for data in transit
- **Access Controls**: Role-based access, multi-factor authentication
- **Monitoring**: 24/7 security monitoring and threat detection
- **Audits**: Regular security assessments and penetration testing
- **Employee Training**: Privacy and security training for all staff

**Important**: No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

---

## Data Retention

We retain your information:

- **Active Accounts**: While your account is active
- **Inactive Accounts**: 2 years after last activity
- **Transaction Records**: 7 years (legal requirement)
- **Deleted Content**: 30 days in backup systems

After retention periods expire, data is securely deleted or anonymized.
